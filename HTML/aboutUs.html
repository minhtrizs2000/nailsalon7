About Us

Nail salon was founded back in 2008. Tired of working in the traditional nail salons, she decided to venture off on her own. Our mission at nail spas is to create a delightful retreat from the pressing worries of the modern world. Embodying our own signature blend of natural and organic ingredients. At Nouvelle, each guest is an individual, and each treatment, a highly personal affair. Our technicians are constantly attuned, sensitive and responsive to your every need. Under their intuitive touch, simple sensory pleasures are reawakened, completing the experience that defines Nouvelle. We’d like to think, having a perfect manicure can also make you feel like your best self.

When visiting nail and spas, you can rest at ease when receiving a treatment. Proper sanitation is the utmost importance. You’ll no longer have to be afraid and second guess on cleanliness. All metal implements are sterilized in a hospital grade AUTOCLAVE for a minimum 20 min. and then sealed in a pouch until next use. You may choose to take the hand file, buffer, and orange wood stick home or you may also choose to purchase your own personal metal implements here at Nouvelle.
